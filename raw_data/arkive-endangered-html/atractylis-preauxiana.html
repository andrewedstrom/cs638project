GenusAtractylis (1)
Top facts
  • Atractylis preauxiana is a shrub species endemic to the Canary Islands, Spain.
  • Atractylis preauxiana has a very fragmented population in an area of occupancy covering just 14  square kilometres.
  • Flowering of Atractylis preauxiana occurs year-round, with ants acting as pollinators.

Atractylis preauxiana is classified as Endangered (EN) on the IUCN Red List (1).

Saharan cypress (Cupressus dupreziana)

French: Cyprès de Duprez, Cyprès de l'Atlas
KingdomPlantae
PhylumTracheophyta
ClassConiferopsida
OrderConiferales
FamilyCupressaceae
GenusCupressus (1)

Classified as Endangered (EN) on the IUCN Red List (1). Varieties: The Atlas cypress or Moroccan cypress, Cupressus dupreziana atlantica is classified as Endangered (EN) and Cupressus dupreziana duprezianais is classified as Critically Endangered (CR) on the IUCN Red List (1).
